Moroccan Beans (Loubia)

  • 500 g dry white beans (soaked overnight and drained - do not use canned beans)/li>
  • i preserved lemon (or organic lemon boiled in salt) cut in strips and seeds removed
  • 3 ripe grated tomatoes
  • 1 medium grated onion
  • 3 cloves smashed garlic
  • 6 T fresh finely chopped flat parsley
  • 6 T fresh finely chopped cilantro
  • 3-6 celery leaves
  • 2 t paprika
  • 2 t fresh ginger
  • 1T salt and pepper
  • optional 1/2 t cayenne pepper or harissa to taste and 1/4t tumeric, 1T tomato paste to add color

step 1

Sauté onions and garlic first until onions are translucent.Add parsley and lemon strips and spices and cook 3 minutes longer. Add a little water and purée this mixture in whole or in part and add vegetable bouillon cube if desired and add 2 liters of water.

step 2

Bring to pressure, then cook under pressure over medium heat for about 40 minutes or until beans are tender

step 3

As soon as each quenelle is cooked and has risen to the surface, remove it with a slotted spoon and place it in a strainer lined with paper towels. Continue until all the quenelle batter has been poached

step 4

(Optional or for reheating) Serve with chopped parsely and cilantro and boiled salty lemon strips
